Origin

December 6, 2022

Two engineers. One vision. The idea emerged during a decisive moment—watching Brazil dominate South Korea 4-1 in the World Cup Round of 16. As we witnessed perfect execution on the field, we recognized our own potential to create something exceptional in technology.

After completing our Master's degrees, we identified a critical gap in the market: businesses struggling with complex technical challenges but lacking access to sophisticated, tailored solutions.

Aidgorithm represents our philosophy—combining the problem-solving precision of algorithms with the supportive nature of aid. We don't just build technology; we architect solutions that fundamentally improve how organizations operate.

Our mission is precise: enable businesses to eliminate inefficiencies, reduce operational complexity, and focus resources on strategic growth rather than technical obstacles.

We believe technology should be invisible, powerful, and purposeful—working seamlessly behind the scenes to amplify human potential and drive meaningful progress.
